Drill Zone

Train with purpose using our professional automated ball machine. The Drill Zone fires shuttlecocks and pickleballs at adjustable speed, spin, and trajectory — cycling through left corner, center, deep backcourt, and cross-court targets so every session challenges your movement and stroke. Ideal for warm-up routines, footwork ladders, return-of-serve practice, and building muscle memory through pure, focused repetition. No partner required — just you and the machine.

FAQ

We are open daily from 7:00 AM to 12:00 AM (midnight). Peak hours are 2:00 PM – 12:00 AM on weekdays and all day on weekends. Courts can be booked up to 7 days in advance.

Online bookings require a reservation deposit via GCash or Credit/Debit Card. The remaining balance is settled at the venue. Walk-in bookings (via front desk) can be paid in cash. We do not store card details.

• Within 15 minutes of booking: Free cancellation, full refund
• 24+ hours before session: 100% credit to your account
• 2–24 hours before session: 50% refund
• Less than 2 hours before session: No refund

Read the full cancellation policy →

No equipment required. We offer racket rental, pickleball paddles, and shoe rental at the venue. You can add equipment rental when booking online. Shuttlecocks and pickleballs are available for purchase at the front desk.

Open Play sessions are drop-in games where players of all levels join together on shared courts. It's the perfect way to meet the community, find playing partners, and enjoy affordable court time without a reservation. View upcoming sessions →

Yes, a free account is required for online bookings so we can keep your reservation history and send confirmation codes. Sign up in under 2 minutes → Walk-in bookings at the front desk do not require an account.

We have 9 indoor courts total: 4 Pickleball courts, 4 Badminton courts, and 1 Drill Zone. All courts are climate-controlled with professional flooring and lighting. Tennis courts are coming in Q4 2025.
