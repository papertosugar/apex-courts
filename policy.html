FAIR BOOKING
POLICY

Our booking policy is designed to ensure fair access to peak-hour courts for all members. Goals: prevent court monopolization, improve access for new members, and maximize court utilization.

Last updated June 2026

Booking Window

Booking Window

Basic Rules
  • Bookings open 7 days before the session start time.
  • Members only.
  • Payment or credits are deducted immediately upon booking.

24-Hour Release Rule

24-Hour Rule

Peak limits lifted 24 hours before session start
From 24 hours before the session start, peak-hour booking limits are lifted. Any remaining courts can be booked by members who have already reached their weekly limit.
Example
📅 Member A: Tue/Thu weekday peak ×2 + Saturday weekend peak ×1 — all limits used
✅ If a Sunday 18:00 court is available and within 24 hours of start, additional booking allowed
Waitlist System

Waitlist System

When a time slot is fully booked, you can join the waitlist. Cancellations are handled automatically.

Automatic Process
1 Automatic notification sent to the first person on the waitlist
2 Payment must be completed within the designated window
3 If unpaid, the slot moves to the next person on the waitlist
