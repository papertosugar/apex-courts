Player Guidelines

Cancellation &
Credit Policy

Effective June 2025 · Last updated June 4, 2025

Refund & Credit Schedule
Cancellation Window Credit Refund Notes
More than 24 hours before 100% Full credit returned
12 – 24 hours before 50% Half credit returned
6 – 12 hours before 30% Partial credit returned
Less than 6 hours before 10% Minimal credit returned
No-show 0% Non-refundable, non-transferable
How to cancel: Log in to your account and cancel from your booking history, or contact the front desk directly. Cancellations must be submitted before the cutoff window — time is calculated from the confirmed booking start time.
Credit Terms
  • Credits are valid for 90 days from the date of issue.
  • Credits may be applied to future court bookings, equipment rentals, and eligible Court Lab services.
  • Credits are non-transferable and may not be converted to cash.
  • Multiple credits may be combined toward a single booking.
  • Credits issued as part of a promotional offer may have separate terms and expiry dates.
Facility Disruptions
  • Apex Courts reserves the right to provide a full refund or full credit in cases of facility issues, safety concerns, or operational disruptions that prevent a session from proceeding.
  • In the event of a partial disruption (e.g., early court closure), a pro-rated credit will be issued for unused time.
  • Disruptions caused by weather, power, or force majeure events will be handled on a case-by-case basis.
No-Shows & Late Arrivals
  • No-show reservations are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Courts will be held for 15 minutes past the booking start time. After that, the court may be released to walk-in players.
  • Late arrivals do not extend the booking end time — the session ends at the originally confirmed time.
  • If you know you'll be late, please call the front desk — we will hold the court when possible.
Booking Modifications
  • Bookings may be rescheduled up to 12 hours before the session start time without penalty, subject to availability.
  • Rescheduling within 12 hours will incur a rescheduling fee of ₱100.
  • Court type changes (e.g., pickleball to badminton) are treated as a cancellation and re-booking.
  • Duration extensions must be requested at the front desk and are subject to court availability.
Questions? Our front desk team is available daily from 7:00 AM to 12:00 AM. Call us at (555) 123-4567 or visit in person.
